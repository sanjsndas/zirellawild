    • Privacy Policy

    We believe in transparency — just like a well‑tended garden. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit Zirella Wild Flora.

    Last reviewed: June 2026 Your data matters to us

    This Privacy Policy was last updated on 30 June 2026.

    1. Who we are

    Zirella Wild Flora (referred to as “we”, “us”, or “our”) is a small independent publication dedicated to gardening, wild flora, and ecological storytelling. Our website is zirellawildflora.com.

    2. Information we collect

    We collect information in two ways:

    • Voluntarily provided: When you fill out our contact form, subscribe to our newsletter, or leave a comment, we may ask for your name, email address, and any message you choose to share.
    • Automatically collected: Like most websites, we use cookies and similar technologies to understand how visitors interact with our site. This includes your IP address, browser type, pages viewed, and referring URLs. We do this to improve your experience and our content.
    Our promise: We never sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Ever.

    3. How we use your information

    We use the information we collect to:

    • Respond to your messages and inquiries.
    • Send you occasional updates (if you’ve opted in).
    • Analyse site traffic and improve our content and design.
    • Protect our website from spam and abuse.

    We do not use your data for automated decision‑making or profiling.

    4. Cookies & tracking

    We use minimal, privacy‑first cookies. These include:

    • Essential cookies: Necessary for basic site functionality.
    • Analytics cookies: We use a self‑hosted or privacy‑respecting analytics tool (no third‑party tracking).
    • Preference cookies: To remember your settings (e.g., dark mode preference).

    You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. For more details, see our Cookies Policy.

    5. Third‑party services

    We do not use third‑party advertising networks. We may link to external services (e.g., podcast platforms, social media) — those services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

    6. Data security

    We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including:

    • Using HTTPS encryption across our entire site.
    • Limiting access to personal information to only those who need it.
    • Regularly reviewing our security practices.

    However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we do our utmost to protect your information.

    7. Your rights

    Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

    • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
    • Withdraw consent at any time (where we rely on consent).
    • Object to or restrict certain types of processing.
    • Request a copy of your data in a portable format.

    To exercise any of these rights, simply reach out to us — we’ll respond within 30 days.

    8. Children’s privacy

    Our site is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us and we will remove it promptly.

    9. Changes to this policy

    We may update this policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. We’ll always post the latest version here with a revised “last updated” date. We encourage you to review it from time to time.

    10. Contact us

    If you have any questions, concerns, or just want to say hello, we’d love to hear from you:

    • Email: hello@zirellawildflora.com
    • Contact form: Visit our contact page
    • Mail: Wild Flora HQ · 42 Meadow Lane, VT
    Thank you for trusting us with your data. We’re committed to keeping your information safe and respecting your privacy — just as we respect the natural world we write about.
