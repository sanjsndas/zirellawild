Terms of Privacy

Your privacy is essential to us. This page explains how Zirella Wild Flora collects, uses, and protects your personal data. We believe in transparency, minimal data collection, and your right to control your information.

Privacy policy summary

Effective date: April 1, 2026

Zirella Wild Flora („we“, „us“, „our“) respects your privacy. This policy describes how we handle your personal information when you visit our website, subscribe to our newsletter, or interact with our content.

What data we collect

  • Email address: if you subscribe to our Flora Dispatch newsletter.
  • Anonymous analytics: we use a privacy‑friendly, self‑hosted analytics tool that does not use cookies or track you across the web. We only see aggregated page views.
  • Comments (future): if we enable comments, we may collect your name and email (only with your explicit consent).

We do not collect IP addresses, browser fingerprints, or any sensitive data. We do not use third‑party trackers, advertising networks, or social media pixels.

How we use your data

  • To send you our newsletter (only if you opt‑in). You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in each email.
  • To improve our content based on aggregate reading patterns (e.g., which articles are most read).
  • To respond to your inquiries if you contact us directly.

We never share, sell, or rent your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for targeted advertising or profiling.

Data storage & security

  • Email addresses are stored in a secure, encrypted database hosted in the EU.
  • We use industry‑standard security measures (SSL, firewalls, regular audits).
  • We retain your email address only as long as you remain subscribed. If you unsubscribe, we delete your email within 30 days.

Your rights

  • Access: request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: update or correct your information.
  • Erasure: ask us to delete your data („right to be forgotten“).
  • Object: opt‑out of any processing (including newsletters).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@zirellawildflora.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Cookies

We use minimal, necessary cookies only for functionality (e.g., remembering your cookie preferences). We do not use tracking, analytics, or advertising cookies. You can manage your cookie settings via your browser.

For more details, see our Cookies Policy.

Third‑party links

Our content may include links to external websites (e.g., botanical gardens, research papers). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

Children’s privacy

Zirella Wild Flora is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If we become aware of such data, we will delete it promptly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will notify you of any material changes via our newsletter or a banner on our website. The latest version will always be available on this page.
