    • Cookies Policy

    Like a gentle breeze through the meadow, we use cookies to make your experience smoother — while always respecting your privacy. This policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can control them.

    Last reviewed: June 2026 Minimal & transparent

    This Cookies Policy was last updated on 30 June 2026.

    1. What are cookies?

    Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences, improve functionality, and provide anonymised analytics. Think of them as little notes that help us tend to your experience.

    2. How we use cookies

    At Zirella Wild Flora, we use only necessary and privacy-conscious cookies. We never use third‑party tracking or advertising cookies. Here’s the full list:

    Cookie name Purpose Duration Type
    zirella_session Maintains your session and basic site functionality (e.g., form state). Session Essential
    zirella_pref Stores your preferences such as dark/light mode or font size. 1 year Preference
    zirella_analytics Used for our self‑hosted, privacy‑first analytics (no external sharing). 30 days Analytics
    zirella_consent Remembers your cookie consent choice. 6 months Essential
    Your privacy comes first: We do not use any cookies from third‑party advertisers, social media trackers, or cross‑site tracking. Our analytics are anonymous and do not collect personal data.

    3. Your control over cookies

    You are always in control. You can:

    • Accept or decline non‑essential cookies via our consent banner (which appears on your first visit).
    • Change your browser settings to block or delete cookies entirely. Every browser is different, but you can usually find these settings under “Privacy” or “Preferences”.
    • Opt out of analytics by disabling the zirella_analytics cookie through your browser.

    Please note that blocking essential cookies may affect the functionality of the site (e.g., forms or login areas).

    4. Third‑party links

    We sometimes link to external resources (like podcasts, articles, or social media). Those sites may use their own cookies, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies. We have no control over third‑party cookies.

    5. Updates to this policy

    We may update this Cookies Policy occasionally to reflect changes in technology or regulations. The latest version will always be posted here with a revised “last updated” date. We’ll also notify you via our website if any significant changes are made.

    6. More information

    If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please don’t hesitate to get in touch. We’re happy to clarify anything.

    A note from our garden: We believe in simplicity and transparency. Our cookies are here to make your visit pleasant — not to track you. Thank you for being part of our community.
