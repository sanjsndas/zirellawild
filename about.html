Our story & team

Started in a coastal garden

Zirella Wild Flora began as a handwritten notebook in a foggy corner of Northern California. Over time, it grew into a collaborative project with ecologists, herbalists, and permaculture designers. We are not academics — but we read deeply and observe relentlessly.

The garden is the oldest library, and we are still learning to read.

Guiding principles

🧑‍🌾

Observation first

Every recommendation begins with what you see, smell, and feel in your own place.

🔄

Cycles, not fixes

We avoid quick fixes — instead we work with succession, decay, and regeneration.

🌍

Bioregional humility

What works in one region may fail in another. We honor local ecotypes.

📚

Slow knowledge

We publish long‑form, deeply researched pieces — no algorithm churn.
